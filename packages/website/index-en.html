🧭 Step-by-step brewing guide: 3 paths, one objective

Pick your method and level; Brasse-Bouillon guides every critical step through fermentation.

Classic all-grain

  • Water prep + pH + milling
  • Mash schedule, sparging, gravity checks
  • Boil, hop additions, cooling
  • Fermentation, tracking, packaging

BIAB (Brew In A Bag)

  • Fast BIAB setup with safety prompts
  • Temperature and extraction guidance
  • Simplified hop schedule
  • Anti-miss checklist through cold crash

Kits / Extracts (beginners)

  • Easy start without unnecessary jargon
  • Guided volumes, dilution, sanitization
  • Timing and key temperature cues
  • First successful batch with less stress

✅ Contextual reminders, required steps, and quality checkpoints from start to bottling.

❓ Quick FAQ

When will the app be available?

We ship in phases. A beta will be shared as soon as the core is stable.

Can I contribute?

Yes—through the questionnaire, UX feedback, and direct contact.

Who is it for?

Beginners and experienced homebrewers alike.

iOS, Android… or both?

The product target is availability on both platforms. Rollout will be progressive, starting with a beta phase.

Will my recipes and batches be saved?

Yes. The product direction includes structured recipe/batch history with persistence, so it is easier to track and reproduce brew sessions.

How can I follow upcoming updates?

The roadmap and development log are continuously updated on the website, with a traceable history of delivered improvements.
