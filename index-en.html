Brasse-Bouillon

A mobile app built for homebrewers—from first batches to advanced recipes. Design, optimize, archive, and share your creations in a friendly and accessible experience.

🍻 What you’ll be able to do

✅ Create and manage your recipes easily
✅ Calculate IBU, ABV, and yield
✅ Plan and track each brewing session
✅ Keep a clear history of every batch
✅ Share your creations with the community
