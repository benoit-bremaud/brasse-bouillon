Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file stored on your device to remember information (e.g., preferences, audience measurement).

2. Cookies used on Brasse-Bouillon

Brasse-Bouillon does not use any tracking cookies or third-party audience measurement tool. No browsing data is collected for analytics purposes.

Forms (newsletter, questionnaire, contact) embedded via Formspree may rely on the provider’s own technical mechanisms to transmit messages. No advertising or profiling cookie is set by the website itself.

3. Legal basis

Since the website sets no non-essential cookies, no prior consent is required for basic browsing. Forms collecting personal data (email, message) remain subject to explicit consent via a dedicated checkbox at submission, in line with GDPR.

4. Managing browser preferences

You can configure your browser at any time to block or delete residual third-party cookies (e.g., those served by the host). No specific Brasse-Bouillon mechanism is required.

5. Contact

For any cookie-related question, use the site form.
