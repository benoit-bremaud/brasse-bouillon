## What Brasse-Bouillon is

- Brasse-Bouillon is a homebrewing companion app that helps beginners brew their own beer,
  step by step, so they never get stuck mid-brew.
- It is educational first: it teaches, so a novice can learn to brew on their own, with help
  always one tap away.

## What it helps with

- The whole beginner journey: choosing or adapting a recipe, preparing, brew day, and bottling.
- Your equipment drives the plan — the app sizes the batch and plans volumes from the gear you
  actually own.
- It keeps a personal "carnet" of recipes and tracks your brewing batches.
